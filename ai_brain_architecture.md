# AI Loyalty Brain Architecture

## Layers
1. **Data Sources**: Transactions, CRM, Engagement, Feedback, Chat, App Behavior
2. **Data Processing**: Feature Engineering, Customer Behavior Profiles
3. **AI Models**: Churn Prediction, Reward Preference, Engagement Prediction
4. **Decision Intelligence**: Emotional Loyalty Index, Reward Engine, Campaign Optimization
5. **Output**: Personalized Rewards, Customer Insights, Campaign Recommendations

## Emotional Loyalty Index (ELI)
```
ELI = 0.35 × Purchase Frequency 
    + 0.25 × Engagement Score 
    + 0.20 × Sentiment Score 
    + 0.20 × Brand Interaction Score
```
